# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the **Shoaib Aftab CSS Framework** community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Professional Standard
This is an enterprise-grade repository. We expect all interactions, pull requests, and code reviews to maintain the highest level of professional courtesy. 

- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy towards other community members.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.

## Zero Dependency Enforcement
As part of our technical conduct, pushing any PR that introduces an external Node.js dependency, third-party CDN, or compromises the zero-dependency architecture is strictly prohibited and will be rejected. 

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at conduct@shoaib-aftab-tech.com.
