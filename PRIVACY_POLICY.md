# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2026

At Shoaib Aftab Tech, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. 

The **Shoaib Aftab CSS Framework** is designed from the ground up to be **100% GDPR, CCPA, and ePrivacy Compliant**.

## 1. Zero Tracking, Zero Data Collection
Our CSS framework is entirely static. It contains **no tracking pixels**, **no analytics scripts**, and **no telemetry data collection**. By using our framework, you leak zero data to us.

## 2. No Third-Party CDNs
To ensure absolute privacy, we have strictly prohibited the inclusion of third-party assets within the core framework.
- **No Google Fonts:** We do not automatically inject `fonts.googleapis.com`. The framework defaults to blazing-fast native system fonts (San Francisco, Segoe UI, Roboto) to prevent Google from collecting your users' IP addresses.
- **Self-Hosting First:** We strongly advise enterprise users to self-host custom `.woff2` files rather than relying on external services.

## 3. Contact
For privacy inquiries, please contact the Shoaib Aftab Tech security team.
